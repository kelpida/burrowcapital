Who We Are

Burrow Capital brings decades of operating and investing experience to early-stage founders building across B2B fintech and commerce enablement.

Laying the infrastructure for the future of fintech

We are at the precipice of the fintech revolution. For new, modern fintech use cases to exist, a new generation of fintech infrastructure needs to be developed. As we look at the consumer fintech successes over the last decade, several have been built on legacy infrastructure that is nearly two decades old. Imagine what could be built on top of a modern infrastructure stack.
‍
With new infrastructure, businesses can finally overcome the biggest hurdles in today’s financial infrastructure: access, utility, flexibility, and cost.
‍
Intensifying what we began at PayPal, our experience offers a unique blend of deep relationships, operational know-how, and understanding of this complex, highly regulated industry. We are creating the foundation necessary for the future of fintech.
